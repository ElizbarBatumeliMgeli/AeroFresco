Section 02

Camera and Vision Data

AeroFresco is a spatial drawing application. To track your hand gestures it needs access to your device camera. Here is exactly how that process works:

  • The camera feed is processed completely locally on your device in real time.
  • The app extracts structural coordinates for your finger joints to move the digital brush.
  • The video frames are immediately discarded from your device memory.

No video or image data is ever recorded, saved to your device disk or sent over the internet.

Data Storage

Because the app does not collect your personal information there are no external servers or cloud databases. Everything you draw stays right on your screen. If you want to keep your artwork you can manually save it to your local photo library using standard iOS and macOS functions.
